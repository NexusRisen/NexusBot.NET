# Privacy Policy for Serena (Discord Bot)

**1. What data we collect**
When you interact with Serena, we may collect and store your Discord User ID and Discord Username. We also temporarily read and log the contents of messages that directly invoke our bot commands (e.g., messages starting with our prefix) or messages where the bot is pinged.

**2. Why we need this data**
*   **User IDs:** Used to manage our automated trade queue, maintain server moderation ban lists to prevent abuse, and track bot usage statistics (such as the number of trades completed for our medals system).
*   **Message Content:** Used strictly to parse and execute the commands you request. We temporarily log command messages to troubleshoot software errors and fix crashes.

**3. How we store the data**
All data is stored securely on an OS-encrypted server behind strict firewalls. Your data is never shared with any third parties, sold, or used for advertising. We do not use your message content to train AI models. Message content is only kept temporarily in local debugging logs, which are periodically purged.

**4. Opting Out and Data Deletion**
Because Serena only processes messages that use specific command prefixes, you inherently opt out of message processing simply by not using the bot. If you wish to have your Discord User ID, username, and statistics permanently removed from our databases and log files, please contact the bot owner or server administrators via Discord, and your data will be manually deleted upon request.
